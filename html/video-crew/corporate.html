Corporate Sector

The Video Crew have a deserved reputation for their work with small businesses and co-operatives, where they have achieved low cost, high quality productions to suit their customers’ needs on a smaller budget. They have also created productions, for some of the largest retail and engineering companies, including branded DVD training products for J Sainsbury’s, Carphone Warehouse, Sony, and many other large organisations. Some of The Video Crew's highly respected films have been showcased in the Houses of Parliament before being distributed nationally and internationally in many languages.
