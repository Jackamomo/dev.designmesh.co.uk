Medical Sector

Education Training

As well as working with colleges and universities in the Education sector we have produced a large amount of work in Medical Education and Training.

The Video Crew - Medical has created over 500 productions to enhance medical training and revision for junior doctors. The subjects we have covered include Clinical Anatomy, the Medical MRCP Clinical Examination (PACES) and the surgical MRCS OSCE.

As training in good clinical practice, we have produced generic aids to communication and history taking, and also a full series of short films on practical skills ranging from venepuncture and catheterisation to suturing and the insertion of chest drains.

The Video Crew - Medical is particularly proud of the feedback received from patients, and doctors.

The Video Crew - Medical is able to take on work dealing with the whole range of medical and paramedical scenarios from clinic-based interviews to operative procedures. In addition we offer a full range of audiovisual products including animation and website design to complement our video productions.

The Video Crew have also created a number of productions featuring people with learning difficulties and/or behavioural difficulties.
