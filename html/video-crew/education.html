Education Sector

At The Video Crew we are at our best working with people, and have a fabulous reputation for humour and affability. We are highly accomplished at filming in sensitive conditions, where we are able to create an easy comfortable atmosphere.

The Video Crew have worked on projects both national and local at all levels of the education system from nursery to higher education including films for OCN, colleges, universities and national teacher training. We have delivered many productions for health and educational projects including those featuring people with learning difficulties and/or behavioural difficulties. We have also designed and run sophisticated media training workshops.
