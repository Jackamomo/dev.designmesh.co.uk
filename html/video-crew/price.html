Price Promise

The Video Crew Ltd we believe in working in partnership with you to acheive the best most cost effective solution to your communication needs. It is important that we produce the video that you require, that you have played an active role in producing and the production is within your budget.

How do we do this?

Use What Works
Some video producers having invested in a new studio, DigiBeta cameras or the latest computer generated whirligig will then when approached by a client want to convince that client that what they really need for their production is... yes you’ve guessed it... A digital video production in a high tech studio using DigiBeta cameras and editing using the very latest computer generated whirligig.

Sensible Approach
We believe the sensible approach is to listen to your needs and then using our years of experience recommend the type of production to suit the message, the method of delivery and your budget.

Work not Profit
The Video Crew was set up to provide quality video production at a fair price, and to keep our technicians camera operators employed. We pay our staff an honest wage and invest in the best digital video equipment we do not add further costs to a clients budget to create profit. This saving is passed on to you.

Keeping Production Cost Low
By not having huge offices and studios within the city. It may feel good operating from a huge Georgian building next to the cappuccino café but the costs of this would add to your production budget.

20% Lower Budget
Our quotes and working budgets are usually 20% lower than our nearest competitors... yes we check... and we always try to ensure that the production comes in on budget even if we have to put extra time into the final twiddlybits.
